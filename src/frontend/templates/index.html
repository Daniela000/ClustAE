ClustAE

Statifying ALS Patients

Patient stratification enhances the understanding of disease heterogeneity and can support clinicians to tailor interventions to individual patients. ClustAE is a deep learning-based stratification method designed to process longitudinal and/or static patient data. Using an autoencoder architecture, it identifies latent patterns in the data, which are subsequently clustered to uncover distinct patient subgroups. We present a pretrained ClustAE model for ALS data, enabling the assignment of progression groups to individual patients based on their clinical characteristics.
